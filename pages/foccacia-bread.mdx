---
title: Foccacia Bread
type: snack
time: 25 mins
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1609501677229-6de2950fc6e7
ingredients:
  - 1 pkg. pizza dough
  - 2 oz. olive oil
  - 1 1/2 oz. kalamata olives (optional)
  - 1 oz. minced garlic
  - 2 tsp. chopped rosemary
  - 2 tbsp. parmesan cheese
  - 1 pinch of salt
---

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°
1. Roll out pizza dough flat without a rim (approx. 10-in circle)
1. Cover entire surface with olive oil
1. Evenly spread garlic and olives across dough
1. Sprinkle rosemary, cheese, and salt
1. Bake on pizza stone until golden brown
1. Allow to cool and serve
