---
title: Fried Cinnamon Strips
type: snack
time: 15 mins
servings: 5 dozen
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1531749668029-2db88e4276c7
ingredients:
  - 1 cup sugar
  - 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
  - 1/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
  - 10 (8-in) flour tortillas
  - cooking oil
  - large resealable plastic bag
---

1. In a large resealable plastic bag, combine sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg; set aside
1. Cut tortillas into 3in x 2in strips
1. Heat 1 in of oil in  a frying pan to 375 degrees
1. Fry 4-5 strips at a time for 30 seconds on each side or until golden brown
1. Drain on paper towels
1. While still warm, place strips in bag with sugar mixture and shake gently to coat
1. Serve immediately or store in airtight container
