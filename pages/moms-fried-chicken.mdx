---
title: Mom’s Fried Chicken
type: meal
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1626082896492-766af4eb6501
ingredients:
  - Canola oil
  - 4 boneless/skinless chicken breasts
  - 4 cups of flour
  - 1 1/2 cups of Bisquick
  - 1/2-3/4 cup of italian breadcrumbs
  - 2 tbsp lemon pepper
  - 1 tbsp Lawry’s seasoned pepper
  - 1 tbsp sea salt
  - 6 eggs - whisked
  - 1 pinch salt
  - 1 pinch pepper
---

#### Dredge:

- mix flour, bisquick, breadcrumbs, lemon pepper, seasoned pepper, and salt in a large bowl or cookie sheet

#### Egg Wash:

- mix eggs, salt, and pepper in a large bowl

#### Chicken:

1. Butterfly chicken breasts (completely / 8 pieces)
1. In a large pan, bring canola oil up to frying temperature
1. Dip chicken in egg wash then dredge, then back into egg wash then dredge again
1. Fry in canola oil
1. Flip about 3 times until golden brown on both sides
1. Remove from pan and let dry on paper towel before serving
