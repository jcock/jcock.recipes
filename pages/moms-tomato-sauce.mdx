---
title: Mom’s Tomato Sauce
type: meal
time: 3.5 hours
servings: 10 servings
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1572441713132-c542fc4fe282
ingredients:
  - 6-10 garlic cloves (finely chopped)
  - 1 shallot (finely chopped)
  - 1 can crushed tomatoes (Cento)
  - 2 cans tomato sauce (Cento)
  - 15 large leaves of fresh basil (rolled and finely chopped)
  - 1/2 tube of basil paste
  - 2 tsp white pepper
  - 1-2 tsp garlic salt (Lawry’s)
  - 2 tsp baking soda
  - 1/2 cup parmesan cheese (shredded or grated)
  - 1/2 lb bonless pork chops (cubed)
  - 1/2 lb beef (cubed)
  - Italian sausage (optional)
  - Olive oil
---

1. Heat large sauce pot
1. Coat bottom of pot with olive oil
1. Add chopped garlic and shallot
1. Sautee until opaque or semi-clear (do not brown)
1. Add pork and beef cubes and optional sausage, 1/2 of pepper, garlic salt, basil paste, and basil leaves
1. Cook meat until all sides are browned
1. Add crushed tomatoes; stir well and scrape boottom of pot
1. Turn heat to med-med/low and simmer for 15 mins, stirring occasionally
1. Add remaining pepper, garlic salt, basil paste, and basil leaves
1. Add tomato sauce; 1 can at a time, every 15 mins and continue to stir
1. Keep sauce at LOW simmer
1. Add lid to pot, tilted to allow steam to escape
1. Cook for 2 hrs at low simmer, adding baking soda halfway through
1. Add parmesan cheese and stir
1. Cook for another 30-45 mins and serve over pasta
