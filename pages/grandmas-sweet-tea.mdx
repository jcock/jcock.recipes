ingredients:
  - 6 qts water
  - 2 1/4 cups sugar
  - 10 tea bags
  - 2 oranges
  - 1 lemon
---

1. Boil 2 quarts of water
1. Turn off burner and add tea bags to steep
1. Put sugar in large bowl
1. Cut fruit in half and squeeze over sugar
1. Strain hot tea into bowl (but keep tea bags in pan), stirring until sugar dissolves
1. Fill pan back up with 4 quarts of cold water and let tea bags steep a little more in cold water
1. Stir in remaining tea to bowl
1. Pour tea over ice into pitchers
