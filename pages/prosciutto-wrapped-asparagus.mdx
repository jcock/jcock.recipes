---
title: Prosciutto-Wrapped Asparagus
type: side
time: 20 mins
servings: 4 servings
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1629875235136-737fef945cfd
ingredients:
  - 20 stalks of asparagus
  - 4 thin slices of prosciutto, fat removed
  - 4 pats (2 tbsp) of butter
  - 1 tsp. freshly ground pepper
  - 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  - 1 lemon, quartered
---

1. Cut off tough ends of asparagus
1. Cover asparagus with water in a skillet
1. Bring to a boil and cook until tender but still firm (about 3-4 minutes)
1. Drain well
1. Divide asparagus into 4 bundles of 3 stalks each
1. Wrap prosciutto slice around center of bundle
1. Place the 4 bundles into oven-safe dish
1. Dot with butter; season with pepper; and sprinkle with parmesan cheese
1. Heat oven to 350 degrees
1. Place in oven to brown cheese (4-5 minutes)
1. Serve with lemon wedges
