---
title: Spring Mix Salad
type: side
time: 5 mins
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1505253716362-afaea1d3d1af
ingredients:
  - 1 bag of spring mix (may substitute with baby spinach leaf or romaine lettuce)
  - 1/2 cup toasted almonds
  - 1 bag dried cranberries
  - 1 can mandarin orange slices
  - 4 oz. crumbled feta cheese
  - 1 tsp. chopped garlic
  - 1/2 cup canola oil
  - 1/2 cup olive oil
  - 1/3 cup cider vinegar
  - 1/4 cup sugar
  - 1 tsp. poppy seeds
  - 1 tsp. dry mustard
---

1. Whisk together garlic, canola oil, olive oil, cider vinegar, sugar, poppy seeds, and dry mustard and chill
1. Mix asll salad ingredients together, add dressing, and serve
