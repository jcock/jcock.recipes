---
title: Steaks
type: snack
time: 35 mins
servings: 12 muffins
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1613119719948-d53865658a88
ingredients:
  - 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  - 3/4 cup sugar
  - 1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  - 1/4 tsp. salt
  - 1/2 tsp. cinnamon
  - 1 large egg
  - 1/2 cup milk
  - 6 tbsp. melted butter
  - 1 1/2 cup blueberries
  - Non-stick cooking spray
---

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
1. Spray 12 muffin cups with non-stick cooking spray
1. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon; mix well
1. In medium bowl, beat egg, milk, and butter
1. Add flour mixture and stir until combined
1. Fold in blueberries
1. Pour batter into muffin cups
1. Bake 25 minutes or until tops are browned
1. During last 10 minutes of baking, sprinkle muffins with sugar
1. Remove from pan and cool slightly on wire rack
