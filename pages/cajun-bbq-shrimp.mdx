---
title: Cajun BBQ Shrimp
type: meal
time: 30 mins
servings: 2 servings
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1592164773751-cbf53a8e5cd1
ingredients:
  - 1/2 c butter
  - 1 tsp. minced garlic
  - 1/2 tsp. ground red pepper (cayenne)
  - 1/2 tsp. freshly ground black pepper
  - 1/2 tsp. thyme
  - 1bsp Worcestershire sauce
  - 12 shelled, deveined uncooked large shrimp
  - 1/2 tsp. rosemary
  - 1/2 tsp. salt
  - 1/2 tsp. fresh lemon juice
  - 4 oz. beer
  - 1/2 tsp. oregano
  - 1/2 tsp. basil
---

1. In medium saucepan, combine butter, garlic, red pepper, black pepper, thyme, oregano, basil, rosemary, salt, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice and beer
1. bring to a boil over medium - high heat
1. Add shrimp
1. stir to coat with sauce
1. Reduce heat to medium
1. simmer 5 to 7 minutes or until shrimp tum pink.
