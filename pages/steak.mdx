---
title: Steak
type: meal
time: 35 mins
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1682334005022-619f67527772
ingredients:
  - 1.5 in thick rib eye steak
  - salt
  - pepper
  - garlic powder
  - onion powder
---

1. Take the steak out of the fridge and let it sit at room temperature for 1 hour
1. Light charcoal in a chimney starter and let it burn until the smoke is clear
1. Season the steak on both sides with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder
1. Place charcoal on one side of the grill
1. Place steak in indirect heat and cook each side for about 10 minutes each or until internal temperature reaches 120°F
1. Remove steak and let rest for 5 minutes
1. Place steak on direct heat and sear for 1-1.5 minutes on each side
1. Remove steak and let rest for 10-15 minutes
1. Steak should reach 130-135°F for medium rare
1. Slice and serve
