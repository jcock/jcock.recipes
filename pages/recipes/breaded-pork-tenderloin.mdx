---
title: Breaded Pork Tenderloin
type: meal
---

## Ingredients:

- 1 whole pork tenderloin
- 1/2 stick butter
- 2 cups olive oil

### Dredge:

- 4 cups of italian breadcrumbs
- 3 cups of shredded parmigiana cheese
- 3/4 cup of flour
- 3 large bay leaves (chopped VERY FINE)
- 1 tbsp of Lawry’s seasoned pepper
- 1/2 tbsp of lemon pepper


### Egg Wash:

- 6 eggs - whisked
- 1 tbsp of water

## Instructions:

1. Prep cookie sheet with parchment paper and set aside
2. Preheat oven to 350°
3. Mix dredge in a large bowl or cookie sheet
4. Mix egg wash in a large bowl
5. In a large pan, bring olive oil and butter up to frying temperature
6. Dip tenderloin in egg wash then dredge, then back into egg wash then dredge again
7. Fry pork, browning on all sides
8. Remove pork from pan and put on cookie sheet
9. Cook in oven until pork is 165° internal temperature (about 30 mins)
10. Slice in 1 in slices and serve
