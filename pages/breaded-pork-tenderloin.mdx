---
title: Breaded Pork Tenderloin
type: meal
time: 40 mins
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1601625618583-1fb9f9d52058
ingredients:
  - 1 whole pork tenderloin
  - 1/2 stick butter
  - 2 cups olive oil
  - 4 cups of italian breadcrumbs
  - 3 cups of shredded parmigiana cheese
  - 3/4 cup of flour
  - 3 large bay leaves (chopped VERY FINE)
  - 1 tbsp of Lawry’s seasoned pepper
  - 1/2 tbsp of lemon pepper
  - 6 eggs - whisked
  - 1 tbsp of water
---

#### Dredge:

- mix italian breadcrumbs, shredded parmigiana cheese, flour, chopped bay leaves, Lawry’s seasoned pepper, and lemon pepper in a large bowl or cookie sheet

#### Egg Wash:

- mix eggs and water in a large bowl

#### Pork:

1. Prep cookie sheet with parchment paper and set aside
1. Preheat oven to 350°
1. In a large pan, bring olive oil and butter up to frying temperature
1. Dip tenderloin in egg wash then dredge, then back into egg wash then dredge again
1. Fry pork, browning on all sides
1. Remove pork from pan and put on cookie sheet
1. Cook in oven until pork is 165° internal temperature (about 30 mins)
1. Slice in 1 in slices and serve
