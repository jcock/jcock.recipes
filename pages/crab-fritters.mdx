---
title: Crab Fritters
type: meal
time: 25 mins
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1601126217456-1744cdad7211
ingredients:
  - 1lb. crab meat
  - 2 eggs
  - 1/2 cup milk
  - 2 cups Bisquick
  - 1 tsp. salt
  - 1 tsp. freshly chopped parsley
  - 1 tsp. Old Bay
  - Vegetable oil
---

1. In medium bowl beat eggs
1. Add milk and Bisquick
1. Blend in salt, parsley, and Old Bay
1. Gently add crab, making sure all meat is coated with batter
1. Heat 3 inches of oil to 350 or 375 degrees in deep pan
1. Drop batter by the tablespoonful in the oil and fry until golden brown
1. Remove and place on paper towels to absorb oil
1. Serve with tartar sauce or cocktail sauce
