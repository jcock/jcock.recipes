---
title: Mussels in White Wine
type: meal
time: 25 mins
servings: 4 servings
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1625430173218-af251641feb9
ingredients:
  - 5 tbsp. olive oil
  - 8 garlic cloves, chopped
  - 1 cup chopped onion
  - 1 tsp. chili powder
  - 2 tbsp. fresh chopped parsley
  - 2 chopped shallots
  - 1 tsp. oregano
  - 4 lbs. mussels, scrubbed and debearded
  - 1 cup white wine
  - 1/4 cup butter
  - juice of 2 lemons
---

1. Heat oil in dutch oven or large pot over medium/high heat until hot
1. Add garlic; sauté 1-2 minutes or until golden
1. Add shallots, onion, chili powder, and oregano; sauté 4-5 minutes or until onion is translucent
1. Stir in mussels, wine, butter, and lemon juice
1. Raise heat to medium/high; steam mussels for 5 minutes
1. Discard any unopened mussels*
1. Garnish with parsley

#### *Tip:

Mussels that do not open during cooking are inedible and should be discarded.
