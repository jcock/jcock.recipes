---
title: Vegetable Pizza
type: meal
time: 25 mins
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1536623236482-d5b70aba5060
ingredients:
  - 2 pkgs. Pillsbury Crescent Rolls
  - 8 oz. sour cream
  - 1 1/2 tbsp. ranch dip mix
  - 2 cups chopped fresh mushrooms
  - 1 cup chopped seeded tomatoes
  - 1 cup chopped small broccoli florets
  - 1/2 cup chopped green peppers
  - 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  - 1/4 tsp. salt
  - 1/8 tsp. pepper
---

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
1. Separate dough into 4 long rectangles
1. Place rectangles crosswise in ungreased 15x10x1 inch baking pan
1. Press over bottom and 1 inch up sides to form crust
1. Seal perforations
1. Bake 14-19 minutes or until golden brown
1. Cool completely
1. Blend sour cream, dip mix, salt, and pepper until smooth
1. Spread evenly over crust
1. Top with remaining ingredients
1. Cut into squares
1. Refrigerate
