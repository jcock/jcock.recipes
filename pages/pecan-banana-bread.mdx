---
title: Pecan Banana Bread
type: snack
time: 75 mins
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1606101273945-e9eba91c0dc4
ingredients:
  - 1/2 cup butter
  - 1 cup sugar
  - 2 eggs
  - 1 tsp. grated lemon peel
  - 2 cups all-purpose flour
  - 3 bananas, mashed (about 1 1/2 cups)
  - 1/2 tsp. salt
  - 1/2 tsp. baking soda
  - 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  - 1 tsp. ground cinnamon
  - 1 cup pecans
---

1. Heat oven to 350Â°
1. Spray 9x5-inch loaf pan with cooking spray
1. In large bowl, combine butter and sugar; beat until well blended
1. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition
1. Beat until fluffy
1. Stir in lemon peel
1. In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon
1. Add dry ingredients to butter mixture alternately with bananas, beginning and ending with dry ingredients
1. Mix just until dry ingredients are moistened; Do not over-mix
1. Stir in pecans
1. Pour into pan
1. Bake 65 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean
1. Cool 10 minutes
1. Remove from pan; cool on wire rack for at least 1 hour before slicing
