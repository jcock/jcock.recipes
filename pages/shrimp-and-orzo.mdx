---
title: Shrimp and Orzo
type: meal
time: 45 mins
servings: 4-6 servings
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1595117796900-e3bb784ea0db
ingredients:
  - 1/2 box orzo (Barilla or De Cecco)
  - 1 lb shrimp (13/15 or 21/25 - peeled and deveined)
  - 1 container grape tomatoes (about 1lb, halved long-ways)
  - 6 lg cloves garlic (finely chopped)
  - 6 lg leaves basil (rolled and finely chopped)
  - 1 tsp white pepper
  - 1 tsp garlic salt (Lawryâ€™s)
  - 1 cup parmesan cheese (shredded)
  - 1 stick butter
  - Basil paste
  - Olive oil
  - Salt
---

1. Bring 1 gallon of water to boil
1. Add 1/4 tsp salt
1. Cook for about 30 mins or until more done then aldente
1. Drain
1. Stir in 2/3 stick of butter, 1 cup of parmesan cheese, and 1/2 tsp of garlic salt, 1/4 tsp white pepper
1. Set aside
1. Chop shrimp into 1/2 (not long-ways)
1. Heat large pan
1. Coat bottom of pan with olive oil
1. Add garlic and sautee until opaque or semi-clear (do not brown)
1. Add tomatoes and sautee
1. Add 1/2 cup of basil, 1.5 tbsp of basil paste, 1/2 tsp garlic salt, 1/2 tsp white pepper
1. Cover and simmer until tomatoes are soft
1. Add shrimp and cook
1. Add 1/4 cup of basil, 1 tbsp of basil paste, 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese
1. Cover and continue cooking shrimp until done (about 5 mins total)
1. Once shrimp is done, let simmer for 2-3 mins

#### Plate:

1. Add orzo to large ceramic dish, lay flat with channel in center
1. Add shrimp and tomato mixture to channel
1. Add 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese on top
