## Ingredients:

- 1 box of angel hair pasta
- 2 lbs large/jumbo shrimp (peeled and deveined)
- 4 cloves of garlic
- 1/4 cup of fresh tarragon
- (2) 6 oz cans of tomato paste
- 1 qt of heavy cream
- 1/2 cup of vodka
- 5 large basil leaves
- 1/2 tbsp fine ground black pepper
- 1/2 tbsp lemon pepper
- 1/2 cup of olive oil

## Instructions:

1. Cook and drain pasta
2. In a large sauce pan, sauté garlic and olive oil
3. Add shrimp
4. Sauté on high
5. Add tarragon, basil, 1/2 of both types of pepper
6. Once shrimp is 3/4 cooked, add vodka
7. Sauté for about 30 sec
8. Add both cans of tomato paste
9. Mix in pasta
10. Add 1/2 of the heavy cream, then slowly stir and continue to add cream to consistency
11. Add the rest of the pepper
12. Turn down heat, let simmer for about 10 mins
