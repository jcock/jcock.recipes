---
title: Spicy Crab Soup
type: meal
time: 2 hrs
servings: 8 servings
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1586735464727-c2d3e4e9a63b
ingredients:
  - 1 qt. water
  - 3 chicken parts (neck or wing)
  - 3 lbs. canned tomatoes, quartered
  - 8 oz. frozen corn, thawed
  - 3/4 tbsp. seafood seasoning
  - 1 lb. fresh Chesapeak crab meat
  - 3/4 cup celery, chopped
  - 3/4 cup onion, diced
  - 1 cup forzen peas, thawed
  - 1 cup potatoes, diced
  - 1 tsp. salt
---

1. Place water and chicken in 6-quart pot
1. Cover and simmer over low heat for at least one hour
1. Add vegetables and seasonings and simmer, covered, over medium-low heat for about 45 minutes, or unitl vegetables are almost done
1. Add crab meat, cover and simmer for 15 more minutes or until hot
