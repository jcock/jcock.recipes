---
title: Tropical Chicken Salad
type: side
time: 5 mins
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1540420773420-3366772f4999
ingredients:
  - 6 boneless chicken breasts
  - 2 small cans of pineapple juice
  - 3/4 can of crushed pineapple
  - 1/2 cup of sliced almonds
  - 1 small shallot, finely chopped
  - 1 tsp. white pepper (may substitute black pepper)
  - 1 cup of Hellmann's mayonnaise
  - 1 tsp. salt
  - whole grain bread
  - 1/4 cup finely chopped celery (Optional)
---

1. Place chicken in deep frying pan
1. Add 2 cans of pineapple juice
1. Steam with lid on until completely cooked
1. Drain chicken and cool in refrigerator
1. Cut chicken into small pieces
1. Put a small amount of canola oil in a small frying pan
1. Add almonds and cook until lightly browned
1. Chop almonds until coarsely chopped
1. Add almonds, shallot, crushed pineapple, and salt and pepper to chicken
1. Mix together
1. Add a 1/3 cup of pineapple juice to mixture
1. Add mayonnaise and mix well
1. Let sit in refrigerator for at least 1-2 hours
1. Check consistency
1. If too dry, add a tablespoon or two of mayonnaise
1. Serve on whole grain bread
