---
title: Pineapple Salsa
type: snack
time: 1 hour
image: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1579639482794-abf71bcd3960
ingredients:
  - 2 1/2 cups fresh pineapple, chopped
  - 1/2 cup red bell peppers, finely chopped
  - 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves, finely chopped
  - 2 tbsp. fresh lemon juice
---

1. Mix all ingredients together

#### Tip:

Can be stored in refrigerator up to 2 days
